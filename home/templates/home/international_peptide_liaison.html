The International Peptide Liaison, IPL, is a consortium of national and international scientific societies united by shared goals in advancing peptide science. The Liaison's primary mission is to foster collaboration among member societies and to enhance the benefits offered to their respective members.

A committee composed of representatives from member societies meets regularly to share updates and to coordinate the schedule for the International Peptide Symposium.

Each year, one member society is selected to host the International Peptide Symposium, held in conjunction with that society's national or regional meeting. The Symposium's program committee is encouraged to include a strong international component to reflect the global nature of the peptide science community.
