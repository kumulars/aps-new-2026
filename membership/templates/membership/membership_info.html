APS Membership

The American Peptide Society brings together scientists from academia, government, and industry who are interested in peptides and proteins.

Membership Benefits

  • Reduced registration fees for the American Peptide Symposium
  • Access to Peptide Science journal at member rates
  • Eligibility for APS awards and travel grants
  • Networking opportunities with leading peptide scientists
  • Regular updates on peptide research and events

Membership Categories

Professional

For scientists actively working in peptide research in academia, industry, or government.

Student

For graduate and undergraduate students engaged in peptide-related research.

Postdoc

For postdoctoral researchers in peptide science.

Retired Professional

For retired members who wish to maintain their connection to the society.

How to Join

Membership in the American Peptide Society is open to scientists with a demonstrated interest in peptide research. New members must either:

  • Have published in the field of peptide science, or
  • Be sponsored by a current APS member
